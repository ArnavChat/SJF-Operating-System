Process Scheduling: Shortest Job First (SJF) in Kitchen

By Sampark, Arnav & Trivikram

Overview

Process scheduling is essential for efficient CPU usage, optimizing the order in which tasks execute to reduce waiting time. Shortest Job First (SJF) scheduling processes tasks with the shortest duration first, minimizing average wait time.

Shortest Job First (SJF) Scheduling

SJF prioritizes tasks with the shortest execution time. In our analogy, we compare this to a kitchen where dishes (tasks) have varying preparation times. The kitchen prioritizes dishes with shorter prep times, ensuring faster service and efficient task handling.
